# Russian Noun Inflections

Russian nouns inflect for number and case, and also have inherent gender.

# Russian Verb Inflections

Russian verbs inflect for tense, mood, person, number, and (in the past tense) gender.

# Russian Pronoun Inflections

Personal pronouns inflect for person, number, gender (where applicable), and case. Gender is only relevant for third-person singular forms.
